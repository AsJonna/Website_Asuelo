MOVIES

Four Sisters and a Wedding

Three sisters reunite to dissuade their younger brother from marrying his fiancee. As they interact, they face the feelings and issues they have kept buried for a very long time.

War Horse

Albert and his beloved horse, Joey, live on a farm in the countryside. At the outbreak of World War I, Albert and Joey are forcibly parted when Albert's father sells the horse to the British cavalry.

Miracle in Cell No. 7

Inmates at a Korean prison join forces to protect a mentally-impaired comrade and his young daughter, after the former is bullied into confessing to a crime he didn't commit.

Wedding Dress

When a single mother learns that she has terminal cancer, she prepares her young daughter to live without her, while saying goodbye to others around her.

Bad Genuis

A genius high school student takes advantage of his abilities to develop elaborate methods to help other students cheat. For a price, of course.

Life of Pi

A teenage Indian boy, Pi, who becomes lost at sea after a shipwreck. Pi finds himself struggling for survival against the torrential ocean with his only companion, a tiger.
